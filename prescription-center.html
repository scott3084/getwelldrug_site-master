FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Where can I obtain a list of the generics offered for $4?
Please click here for a list of the $4 generics offered at your local Getwell Drug & Dollar.

What are the new rules for Over-the-Counter (OTC) drugs and medicines?
For a full description of the New OTC Rules and Facts for Heath Care Account
Participants and when the changes will take place, please click here.

Where can I learn more about Medicare Part D and what it means for me?
Everyone with Medicare is eligible for Medicare Part D prescription drug coverage.
For more details on Medicare Part D, please visit www.medicare.gov.

What insurance plans does Getwell Drug & Dollar accept?
Your Getwell Drug & Dollar accepts most insurance plans including: TRICARE®, Express Scripts,
Blue Cross/Blue Shield, Cigna, Humana, CVS Caremark, and many more, as well as Medicare Part B
and Part D Plans.

90-day prescription supply is available for many plans; just ask your friendly Getwell Pharmacist for
details.
